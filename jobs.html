For getting into product based companies like Google, Microsoft, Amazon and Facebook etc, coding is an important skill to master. GeeksForGeeks and LeetCode are great websites to practice coding and prepare for such product based companies. CodeChef, CodeForces, hackerearth and HackerRank are platforms to practice competitive programming.

Aptitude and Reasoning books by RS Agarwal are the best books for preparing quantitative aptitude and reasoning sections for online tests for these companies, both service and product-based.
